Big Data Platform Proposal – BYD

Architectural blueprint for EV fleet telemetry, focusing on high-volume real-time ingestion and predictive intelligence.

Project Overview

A comprehensive technical proposal addressing the massive telemetry data needs of BYD's expanding EV fleet. This architecture bridged the gap between raw hardware sensor data and actionable business intelligence, focusing on high-volume ingestion and low-latency processing.

Strategic Framework

Key Objectives

  • Scalable Ingestion: Design a pipeline capable of processing 10TB+ of daily telemetry data.
  • Predictive Maintenance: Establish data schemas for real-time battery health diagnostics.
  • Operational Intelligence: Enable sub-second latency for global fleet tracking.
  • Security: Ensure PII data privacy across international borders.

  • KPI Alignment: Mapped core business metrics (e.g., charging efficiency) to analytical modules.
