Statement of Work Healthcare Executive Dashboard

Senior Leadership Dashboard – EHCD

Comprehensive Statement of Work (SOW) defining the scope, governance, and technical delivery of an executive-level digital portal and mobile application.

Statement of Work Summary

Authored the high-level Statement of Work for the development of the "Excellence in Health Care Delivery (EHCD)" Senior Leadership Dashboard.

This blueprint defined the transition of manual project governance into a centralized, bilingual (English/Arabic) digital ecosystem for top-tier healthcare executives.

Technical & Delivery Scope

Core Technical Scope

  • RBAC: Secure data partitioning for sensitive leadership metrics.
  • Dynamic Governance: Real-time tracking of project lifecycle and strategic alignment.
  • Bilingual UI/UX: Full RTL (Right-to-Left) support for Arabic language users.
  • Admin Control: Master management console for system configuration.

Project Delivery Modules

  • Executive Mobile App: Cross-platform app for on-the-go decision support.
  • Web Portal: Desktop interface for detailed data entry and audit trails.
  • Intelligence: Integration of Power BI Embedded for high-fidelity visualization.
  • Service Excellence: Training framework and post-launch maintenance.

Governance & Change Management

Established a rigorous framework to maintain project integrity. This included a tiered approval process for scope variations, formal impact assessment reporting, and a centralized audit trail to ensure accountability during the development lifecycle.

Exclusions & Boundaries

  • Exclusion of education-specific dashboard modules in Phase 1.
  • Limited mobile management (user administration restricted to web portal for security).
  • Defined limits on third-party legacy system integrations to prevent scope creep.
