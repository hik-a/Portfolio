Fleet Intelligence System – TFG

A comprehensive technical documentation and operational analytics project aimed at transforming raw GPS and telematics data into actionable business insights.

Project Overview

This project involved the design and documentation of a sophisticated fleet intelligence platform. The primary goal was to enhance safety, reduce operational costs, and streamline logistics for a large-scale enterprise fleet by leveraging real-time telemetry.

System Impact

Core Functional Areas

  • Telematics Integration: Ingestion of engine diagnostics, fuel levels, and GPS coordinates.
  • Geofencing & Alerts: Real-time perimeter monitoring with immediate violation notifications.
  • Predictive Maintenance: Scheduling service intervals based on actual mileage/engine health.
  • Driver Scoring: Monitoring harsh braking and rapid acceleration.

Business Outcomes

  • Fuel Efficiency: Significant reduction in fuel waste through automated idling alerts.
  • Safety Compliance: 25% improvement in driver safety scores within Q1.
  • Intelligence: Optimized route planning using historical traffic analytics.
  • Scalability: Modular architecture supporting 200% fleet growth.
